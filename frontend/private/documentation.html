1. How It Works: The 3-Step Creation Flow

Follow these simple steps to go from concept to creation in seconds using the Pulix Image Generator.

01

Write Your Prompt

Enter a detailed description of the image you want. Be specific about the subject, setting, and mood. *Example: "A neon cityscape at sunset, vaporwave style."

02

Enter Style and Aspect

Enter in promt a creative style name (e.g., Watercolor, Cinematic, Stylized) and select the desired aspect ratio (e.g., 16:9, 1:1) from the sidebar controls.

03

Generate and Refine

Press 'Generate Image'. The AI will produce your visual.

2. Feature Showcase: Core Tools in Detail

2.1 Advanced Image Generation

Our proprietary AI model, Pulix Engine, ensures photorealistic detail and consistency across all styles.

  • Style Presets: Access over 100 curated styles, including Cyberpunk, Oil Painting, and Minimalist 3D.
  • Negative Prompting: Tell the AI what *not* to include in your image for better control over the final output.
  • Batch Creation: Generate up to 8 variations of the same prompt simultaneously.

2.2 Smart Hashtag & Caption Agent

Optimize your content reach with AI-driven analysis of trending topics **and generate compelling descriptions** for your social media posts.

  • Caption Generation: Automatically creates engaging, tone-specific descriptions for your visuals (e.g., promotional, storytelling, casual).
  • **Trending Mode:** Focuses on current viral hashtags for maximum short-term reach.
  • Evergreen Mode: Generates relevant, long-tail tags that perform well over time.
  • Platform Optimization: Tailored hashtag sets for Instagram, X (Twitter), and TikTok algorithms.
